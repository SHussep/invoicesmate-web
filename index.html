Features

Everything you need to run your business

From the first quote to the final report — invoices, expenses and customers, all in one simple app.

📄

Professional invoices

Create and send polished invoices in seconds. Choose from 5 professionally designed PDF templates. Payment status and overdue tracking happen automatically.

📝

Quotes & estimates

Send professional quotes with custom validity periods. Convert an accepted quote to an invoice with a single tap. Never lose a deal.

💰

Expense tracking

Record business expenses with categories, GST breakdowns and receipt attachments. Keep every dollar accounted for at tax time.

📈

Financial reports

Profit & loss, expense breakdowns, top customers and aged receivables. Export for your accountant in Excel or PDF.

👥

Customer management

Keep a database of your clients with contact details, ABN and transaction history. Quick access when creating invoices or quotes.

📶

Works offline

Full functionality without an internet connection. Your data syncs automatically when you're back online — never miss a beat on the go.

Made for Australia

Tax compliance
built right in

Designed specifically for Australian sole traders and small businesses. GST, ABN and fiscal-year calculations are handled automatically.

✅

GST calculations

Automatic 10% GST on invoices, quotes and expenses. Track GST collected and paid for your BAS reporting.

💲

ABN support

Store your ABN and your clients' ABNs, and display them on invoices as required by Australian tax law.

📅

Australian fiscal year

Reports and dashboards follow the July–June financial year. Filter everything by FY instantly.

⚠️

GST threshold alerts

Get notified when your turnover approaches the $75,000 GST registration threshold.
